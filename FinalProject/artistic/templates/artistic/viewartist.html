What exactly is an artist?


Artists are people who notice things and find significance there, they draw connections. Artists are people who make art as a symbol, a keepsake, a mark of that experience of noticing. By turning this impulse into a practice, an artist develops the skills to craft this work consistently and thoughtfully.
